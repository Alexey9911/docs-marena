Havn virtual cards are fully compatible with modern mobile payment systems. Tokenize your crypto debit card directly into your smartphone for contactless in-store payments.

## Global PoS Usage

Once tokenized, your Havn virtual card can be used at physical Point of Sale (PoS) terminals globally, providing a bridge from your wallet to the physical world with zero cross-border friction.
