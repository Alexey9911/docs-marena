# Ecosystem Tiers

Havn Finance utilizes a tiered ecosystem designed to scale with user needs. Tiers are algorithmically determined based on funding volume.

| Tier | Range | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard** | < $500 | Ideal for daily micro-transactions and digital subscriptions. |
| **High** | $500 - $999 | Enhanced capacity for travel and larger e-commerce purchases. |
| **Max** | ≥ $1000 | The flagship tier for institutional-grade spending and high-volume utility. |

## Scalability

As your funding volume grows, your tier status updates automatically, unlocking higher spending limits and specialized visual credentials.

<Tip>
  High-volume users in the **Max** tier benefit from priority processing and specialized institutional support.
</Tip>
