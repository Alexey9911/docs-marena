# Privacy & Security

At Havn Finance, we prioritize your financial autonomy and privacy. Our infrastructure is designed to minimize data collection while maximizing security.

## Data Autonomy

As a non-custodial platform, we do not require traditional identity verification (KYC). We do not store your private keys or maintain long-term access to your crypto balances.

### Key Policies

* **No Intrusive Tracking:** We do not sell user data to third-party advertisers.
* **Encrypted Provisioning:** Virtual card credentials are delivered via secure, encrypted channels.
* **Smart Contract Security:** All funding transactions are routed through audited infrastructure.

## Terms of Service

By using $Havn Finance, you agree to the decentralized nature of the protocol. Users are responsible for their own wallet security and transaction confirmations.
