# Provisioning Process

Our system is designed for speed and privacy. The provisioning lifecycle happens in three seamless steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Asset & Network">
    Choose your preferred cryptocurrency (SOL, ETH, BTC, or USDC) across supported blockchains:
    * **Solana**
    * **Ethereum**
    * **Polygon**
    * **Base / Optimism**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Smart Contract Routing">
    The protocol generates a unique, single-use deposit address. Upon confirmation, institutional-grade liquidity mappers pick up the transaction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Instant Issuance">
    The deposit is converted with **0% hidden spread**. Your Visa® or Mastercard® credential is provisioned instantly to your secure inbox.
  </Step>
</Steps>
