# The $Havn Protocol

Havn Finance operates as a sovereign, non-custodial gateway. We never hold your private keys or maintain long-term custody of your crypto balances.

## Non-Custodial Architecture

Our infrastructure utilizes smart-contract routing. When you initiate a funding transaction:
1. Liquidity is algorithmically converted.
2. Conversion happens at the exact moment of authorization.
3. A virtual credential is issued directly to your secure environment.

### Core Philosophy

The platform is designed to be **Zero KYC** and **Total Privacy**. No traditional identity verification or intrusive onboarding is required to provision your virtual card.

<Note>
  You remain in absolute control of your funds until the exact moment of spending through our secure smart contract routing.
</Note>
